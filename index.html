IRON KILL

A robot fighting game for PC. A Cosmetopia game.

Build, upgrade, and master a roster of battle robots, then fight your way through a world tour of arenas. Iron Kill is a robot fighting game for PC with full controller support, fight replays, and online battles.

Features

• Dozens of unique fighter robots to collect and upgrade
• Campaign, leagues, and online 1 v 1 battles
• Fight replays with pause, rewind, and scrubbing
• Unlockable profile pictures and achievements
• Full keyboard, mouse, and gamepad support
• No ads — everything is earnable through play
