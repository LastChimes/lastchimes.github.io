// Choose Your Fight

More Than a World Tour

Take on the campaign, build your own match, share the couch, enter the ring, or risk a run through Smith's Gauntlet.

01 // CAMPAIGN

Climb to the Championship

Build and upgrade a roster of battle robots, fight across a world tour of arenas, and defeat 12 campaign bosses.

02 // CUSTOM MATCH

Your Robots, Your Rules

Choose the fighters, arena, difficulty, match rules, and team setup. Practice against AI or shape a fight for the people beside you.

03 // LOCAL MULTIPLAYER

Settle It on the Couch

Connect a second controller for local head-to-head fights, including tag-team battles with multiple robots on each side.

04 // 3D BOXING

Step Into the Ring

Move around a third-person boxing arena, trade punches, block, and win by knockout in an auto-balanced one-on-one fight.

05 // SMITH'S GAUNTLET

Risk the Run

Take one robot through an escalating run of fights. Bank the purse and walk away—or push onward and risk what you have earned.

06 // REPLAYS

Relive Every Hit

Save full fight replays, pause the action, rewind, change speed, and scrub directly to the moment you want to see again.

07 // PC FEATURES

Built for the Way You Play

Play with keyboard and mouse or controller, keep progress through Epic cloud saves, and unlock 12 Epic achievements.
